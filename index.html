|Introduction

Hello, My name is Sergio Chiquete Castro, born and raised in the Bay Area, CA, technology has been an integral part of my life from an early age. I was always eager and excited about the technology all around me, whether it was the family computer, gaming consoles, or the latest gadgets. My curiosity led me to disassemble and reassemble electronics, trying to understand how they worked. These early experiences ignited a passion for technology that has only grown stronger over the years. Now, at 21 years of age, I plan to pursue a career in software development. Throughout my academic journey, I have honed my skills in various programming languages and technologies. I have completed numerous projects that have solidified my understanding of software development principles and practices.
Surrounded by the constant evolution of electronics, I've developed a deep appreciation for the impact of technology on our world. Currently, I'm pursuing an associate's degree in communications alongside a certificate in computer science, with graduation slated for May 2024. Looking ahead, my goal is to transfer to a four-year institution to further explore the dynamic field of computer science. Eager to expand my skills and gain hands-on experience, I'm actively seeking internship opportunities for the summer of 2024. I'm excited to embark on new challenges and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
